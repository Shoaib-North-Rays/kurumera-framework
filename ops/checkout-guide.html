Customize your checkout

Make your checkout page look and feel like the rest of your store — colors, fonts, labels, and which fields appear — right from the builder. No code needed.

The short version.
Your checkout already picks up your brand automatically. To fine-tune it: open the builder → Checkout page → tweak → Publish.

What you can change

Everything below is edited visually — nothing to write.

Colors & fonts

Match your brand. It starts on-brand from your theme — fine-tune the rest.

Field labels

Rename Email, Name, Address, City, Postal code, Country, Phone.

Optional fields

Show or hide Last name, Apartment, Postal code, Country, and Phone.

Headings & button

Edit “Payment method”, “Order summary”, and the “Place order” button.

Do it in 5 steps

About two minutes, start to finish.

  1. Open the Checkout page in the builder

    Sign in to your store, open the builder, and go to the Checkout page.

  2. Select the checkout form

    Click the checkout form block. Its settings appear in the inspector on the right.

  3. Style it to your brand

    Adjust colors, fonts, and spacing in the inspector. It already inherits your theme, so you’re just refining.

  4. Rename labels & toggle fields

    Click any label to edit it inline, or use the inspector to rename fields and switch optional ones on or off.

  5. Publish

    Hit Publish. Your checkout goes live — secure, on your domain, and matching your store.

Why you can’t add your own fields

The field set is fixed to the standard details every order needs. You can restyle, relabel, and show/hide them — but not add custom fields. That keeps every order complete, valid, and reliable to fulfill.
